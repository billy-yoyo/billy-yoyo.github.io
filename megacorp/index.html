MAAS Biolabs

MAAS is a merger of five enormous Avon Corps, bonded to form Avon’s first MegaCorp in the post-parrateraformed stage. M built nuclear armaments, A1 developed Cyber-Security & Malware, A2 dominated Avon’s aerospace market and S was the leading biotech company. Together they form an outfit that is the main supplier of military equipment to both the Protectorate and Avon militaries. Accusations have been leveled that MAAS funds and markets military uprisings in the Colonies to cause surges in demand; however, attempts to prove or investigate this have resulted in unprecedented levels of deaths in journalism. MAAS is currently attempting to take over Juno Aviation’s monopoly in the personal aerospace market. If becomes any closer to reality, the corpo-violence and market impact will be unquantifiable and unquestionably good for business.
